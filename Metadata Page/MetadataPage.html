The Philadelphia Eagles' Facts and Players

Facts about the Philadelphia Eagles

    The Philadelphia Eagles is a National Football League team. The Eagles is located in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. Their stadium they play at is at The Lincoln Financial Field. The coach of the Eagles is Doug Peterson. The Eagles won SuperBowl 52. The game was against the Patriots. The Eagles won the SuperBowl with a score of 41-33. The Eagles first season was in 1933. The Philadelphia Eagles' first win was on November 5, 1933.
